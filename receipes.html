Hot And Sour Soup

Hot and Sour Soup Vegetable Soup is an easy and simple soup recipe known for its spice and sourness taste. It is a fast food or a street food recipe served as appetizer or starter just before a meal. It is made with diced vegetables and Chinese sauces. The best part of this soup is the sourness in its taste which activates the taste buds. Most of the soup offers you either spice or some sort of savoury. But in hot sour soup, it is a combination of spice and sourness and thus makes it an ideal meal starter.
